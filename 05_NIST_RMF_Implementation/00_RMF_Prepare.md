# RMF Prepare Step

## 1. Organizational Context
Q-Day Cloud Services applies the NIST RMF to high-impact cloud systems supporting regulated customers.

## 2. System Selection
The RMF process applies to the Q-Day Cloud Management Platform (CMP), which provisions, manages, and monitors customer cloud environments.

## 3. Stakeholders
- Authorizing Official (AO)
- CISO
- GRC Team
- Cloud Operations
- Compliance and Legal

## 4. Risk Strategy Alignment
RMF implementation aligns with enterprise risk appetite and governance framework.
