# Decision Record: Architecture Trade-Off

## Decision ID
CDR-003

## Context
Choice between single-region vs multi-region cloud deployment.

## Options Considered
1. Single-region (lower cost)
2. Multi-region (higher resilience)

## Risk Analysis
Single-region increases availability risk.

## Decision Taken
Phased move to multi-region architecture.

## Risk Treatment
Mitigate over time

## Approval
CISO & CTO

## Review Date
12 months
