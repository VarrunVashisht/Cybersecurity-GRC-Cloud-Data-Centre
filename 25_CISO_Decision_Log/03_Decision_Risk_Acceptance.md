# Decision Record: Temporary Risk Acceptance

## Decision ID
CDR-001

## Context
Legacy reporting system lacks full MFA support.

## Options Considered
1. Delay launch until MFA upgrade completed
2. Accept risk temporarily with compensating controls
3. Decommission system

## Risk Analysis
- Option 1 delays business-critical reporting
- Option 2 introduces limited access risk
- Option 3 not feasible short-term

## Decision Taken
Option 2 selected with enhanced logging and restricted access.

## Risk Treatment
Accept (temporary, time-bound)

## Approval
CISO & Business Owner

## Review Date
90 days
