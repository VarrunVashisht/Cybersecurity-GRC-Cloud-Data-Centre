# CISO Decision Log Framework

## Purpose
To document significant cybersecurity decisions, including accepted risks, trade-offs, and strategic choices, in a transparent and defensible manner.

## What Gets Logged
- Risk acceptance decisions
- Control deferrals
- Investment prioritization
- Architecture and tooling trade-offs

## Decision Principles
- Business-aligned
- Risk-informed
- Time-bound
- Approved at the appropriate level
