# Cybersecurity Maturity Roadmap – Executive Summary

## Current Position
Q-Day Cloud Services demonstrates a Defined-to-Managed level of cybersecurity maturity across most domains.

## Improvement Strategy
The focus over the next 12–24 months will be on:
- Enhancing third-party risk visibility
- Increasing automation and efficiency
- Improving proactive risk management

## Executive Message
Maturity improvements will be phased and risk-driven to maximize value while minimizing disruption.
