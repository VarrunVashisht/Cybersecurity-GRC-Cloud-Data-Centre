# Cybersecurity Maturity Model – Definition

## Purpose
To assess the maturity of cybersecurity capabilities at Q-Day Cloud Services and guide continuous improvement.

## Maturity Levels

### Level 1 – Initial
- Ad hoc, reactive
- Limited documentation
- Inconsistent execution

### Level 2 – Developing
- Basic processes defined
- Partial consistency
- Limited metrics

### Level 3 – Defined
- Formalized policies and processes
- Consistent execution
- Clear ownership

### Level 4 – Managed
- Metrics-driven
- Proactive risk management
- Regular reviews and improvements

### Level 5 – Optimized
- Continuous improvement
- Automation and integration
- Strong business alignment
