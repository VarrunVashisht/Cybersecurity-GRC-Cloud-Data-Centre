# KPI vs KRI Mapping

| Area | KPI (Performance) | KRI (Risk) |
|----|------------------|-----------|
| Access Control | % MFA coverage | # privileged accounts without MFA |
| Incident Response | Avg response time | # incidents exceeding SLA |
| Risk Management | Risks mitigated | Overdue high risks |
| Third-Party Risk | Assessments completed | High-risk vendors |
