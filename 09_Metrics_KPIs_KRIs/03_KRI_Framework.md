# Key Risk Indicators (KRIs)

## Enterprise KRIs
- Number of high and critical risks
- Percentage of overdue risk treatments
- Third-party high-risk vendors count

## Cloud-Specific KRIs
- Number of public-facing misconfigurations
- Privileged access without MFA
- Logging gaps in critical systems

## Risk Thresholds
KRIs are monitored against predefined thresholds aligned with risk appetite.
