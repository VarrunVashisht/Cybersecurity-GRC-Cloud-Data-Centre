# Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

## Operational KPIs
- Percentage of systems covered by logging
- Patch compliance rate
- Incident response time
- Completion rate of security awareness training

## Strategic KPIs
- Control effectiveness improvement over time
- Reduction in repeat audit findings
- Percentage of risks mitigated within SLA

## KPI Ownership
Each KPI has an assigned owner responsible for accuracy and improvement.
