# Cybersecurity Metrics Strategy

## 1. Purpose
This document defines how cybersecurity metrics are used to measure performance, monitor risk, and support decision-making at Q-Day Cloud Services.

## 2. Objectives
- Provide visibility into security posture
- Enable risk-based decisions
- Support executive and board oversight
- Drive continuous improvement

## 3. Guiding Principles
- Metrics must be relevant and actionable
- Focus on trends, not raw numbers
- Align metrics with business objectives and risk appetite
