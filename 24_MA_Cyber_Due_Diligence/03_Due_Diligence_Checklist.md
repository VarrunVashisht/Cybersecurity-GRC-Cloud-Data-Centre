# Cyber Due Diligence Checklist

## Governance
- Is there a security leader or function?
- Are policies and risk processes documented?

## Technical Controls
- Identity and access management
- Logging and monitoring
- Encryption practices

## Incident History
- Past security incidents or breaches
- Regulatory investigations or fines

## Compliance
- Applicable regulations
- Certifications or audits

## Third-Party Risk
- Critical vendors
- Data sharing arrangements
