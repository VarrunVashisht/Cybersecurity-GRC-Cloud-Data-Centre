# M&A Cyber Due Diligence Overview

## Purpose
To assess cybersecurity risks associated with the acquisition of a target organization and inform executive decision-making.

## Scope
- Governance and risk management
- Security controls and architecture
- Incident history and readiness
- Regulatory and legal exposure
- Third-party and supply-chain risks

## Outcome
Provide a clear view of cyber risks that may impact valuation, integration, or regulatory approval.
