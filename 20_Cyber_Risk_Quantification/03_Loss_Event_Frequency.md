# Loss Event Frequency Estimation

## Frequency Drivers
- Threat landscape trends
- Historical incidents
- Control effectiveness
- Industry benchmarks

## Example Estimates
- Cloud misconfiguration breach: 0.3–0.5 per year
- Privileged access compromise: 0.2–0.4 per year
- Third-party breach: 0.1–0.3 per year

## Notes
Estimates are reviewed annually and adjusted based on control improvements.
