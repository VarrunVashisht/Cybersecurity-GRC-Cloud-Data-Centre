# Control Cost vs Risk Reduction Analysis

## Example Control
Enhanced cloud configuration monitoring

## Cost
- Tooling and implementation: $150k annually

## Risk Reduction
- Reduces breach likelihood from 0.4 to 0.15
- New ALE: $300k per year

## Decision Impact
- Risk reduction value: ~$500k annually
- Net benefit: ~$350k per year

## Recommendation
Proceed with control implementation.
