# Cyber Risk Quantification Approach

## Purpose
To estimate the potential financial impact of cybersecurity risks and support informed business decisions.

## Methodology
Q-Day Cloud Services uses a scenario-based risk quantification approach inspired by FAIR principles.

## Key Components
- Loss Event Frequency (how often)
- Loss Magnitude (how much)
- Annualized Loss Expectancy (ALE)

## Output
Risk estimates are presented as ranges to reflect uncertainty.
