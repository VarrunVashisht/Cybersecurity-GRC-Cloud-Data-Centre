# Cyber Loss Scenarios

## Scenario 1: Cloud Misconfiguration Data Breach
- Public exposure of customer data due to misconfigured storage
- Regulatory notification required
- Customer trust impacted

## Scenario 2: Privileged Account Compromise
- Compromise of admin credentials
- Unauthorized changes to cloud environment
- Temporary service disruption

## Scenario 3: Third-Party Service Breach
- Vendor breach affecting shared data
- Contractual and reputational impact
