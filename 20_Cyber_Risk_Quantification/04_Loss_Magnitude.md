# Loss Magnitude Estimation

## Loss Categories
- Incident response and recovery costs
- Regulatory fines and legal fees
- Customer notification and remediation
- Revenue loss and churn
- Reputational damage

## Example Ranges
- Low impact: $200k – $500k
- Medium impact: $500k – $2M
- High impact: $2M – $8M

## Considerations
Loss magnitude varies by incident scope, response speed, and regulatory exposure.
