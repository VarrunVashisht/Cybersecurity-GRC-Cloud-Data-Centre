# Incident Response Policy

## 1. Purpose
To establish a structured approach for identifying, responding to, and recovering from cybersecurity incidents.

## 2. Scope
This policy applies to all cloud systems, data, employees, and third-party providers.

## 3. Policy Statement
Q-Day Cloud Services will respond to cybersecurity incidents in a timely, coordinated, and legally compliant manner.

## 4. Authority
The CISO is authorized to activate the Incident Response Team and take immediate actions to protect systems and data.
