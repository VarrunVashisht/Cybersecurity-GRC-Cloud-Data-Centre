# Incident Response Team and Roles

## Incident Commander
- Coordinates overall response
- Makes time-critical decisions

## Technical Lead
- Leads investigation and containment
- Works with cloud operations

## GRC & Compliance Lead
- Assesses regulatory impact
- Manages documentation and evidence

## Legal Counsel
- Advises on legal exposure
- Oversees breach notification

## Communications Lead
- Manages internal and external communications
