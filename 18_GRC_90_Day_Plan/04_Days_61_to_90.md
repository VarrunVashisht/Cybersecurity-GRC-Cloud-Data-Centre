# Days 61–90 – Optimize and Lead

## Key Objectives
- Embed GRC into business operations
- Influence strategic decisions
- Set long-term direction

## Activities
- Perform targeted risk reassessments
- Support audits or regulatory interactions
- Lead tabletop exercises or risk workshops
- Define 12–24 month GRC roadmap

## Deliverables
- Strategic GRC improvement plan
- Board-ready risk narrative
- Long-term roadmap

## Success Indicators
- Executives seek GRC input proactively
- GRC seen as business enabler
