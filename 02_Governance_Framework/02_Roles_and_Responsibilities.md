# Roles and Responsibilities

## 1. Board of Directors
- Provide oversight of cybersecurity risk
- Approve cybersecurity strategy and risk appetite
- Review major incidents and risk reports

## 2. Executive Management
- Ensure cybersecurity aligns with business objectives
- Allocate resources for security initiatives
- Enforce accountability across the organization

## 3. Chief Information Security Officer (CISO)
- Develop and maintain the cybersecurity program
- Report cybersecurity risks to executive management and the Board
- Oversee incident response and risk management

## 4. GRC Function
- Maintain governance frameworks and policies
- Conduct risk assessments and compliance activities
- Monitor regulatory and audit requirements

## 5. IT and Cloud Operations
- Implement and operate security controls
- Maintain secure cloud architecture
- Support incident detection and response

## 6. Employees and Contractors
- Follow security policies and procedures
- Report security incidents or suspicious activities
