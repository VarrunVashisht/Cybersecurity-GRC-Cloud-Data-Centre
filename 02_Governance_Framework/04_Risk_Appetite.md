# Cybersecurity Risk Appetite Statement

## 1. Risk Appetite Definition
Q-Day Cloud Services has a low tolerance for risks that:
- Impact customer data confidentiality
- Affect cloud service availability
- Result in regulatory non-compliance

## 2. Acceptable Risk Levels
- Low risks may be accepted with management approval
- Medium risks require documented mitigation plans
- High and critical risks require executive approval or must be mitigated

## 3. Risk Acceptance Authority
- Business unit leaders may accept low risks
- CISO and executives approve medium risks
- Board approval is required for high or critical risks
