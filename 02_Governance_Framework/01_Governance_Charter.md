# Cybersecurity Governance Charter

## 1. Purpose
The purpose of this charter is to establish a formal cybersecurity governance framework that ensures cybersecurity risks are managed in alignment with business objectives, regulatory requirements, and risk appetite.

## 2. Scope
This governance framework applies to:
- All cloud infrastructure and services
- All employees, contractors, and third parties
- All customer and internal data processed by Q-Day Cloud Services

## 3. Governance Objectives
- Ensure accountability for cybersecurity decisions
- Align cybersecurity strategy with business goals
- Enable effective risk management and compliance
- Provide transparency to executive management and the Board

## 4. Governance Principles
- Cybersecurity is a business risk, not just a technical issue
- Risk ownership lies with the business
- Decisions are made based on risk and impact
- Continuous oversight and improvement are mandatory
