# Cybersecurity Escalation and Reporting Model

## 1. Escalation Triggers
- Security incidents impacting customer data
- Regulatory non-compliance
- High or critical risk identification
- Repeated control failures

## 2. Reporting Flow
- Operational teams report to GRC and CISO
- CISO reports to executive management
- Executive management reports to the Board

## 3. Reporting Frequency
- Monthly operational risk reports
- Quarterly executive and board reports
- Immediate escalation for critical incidents
