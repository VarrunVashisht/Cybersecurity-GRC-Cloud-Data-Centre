# Cybersecurity Governance Committees

## 1. Cybersecurity Steering Committee
### Members:
- CISO (Chair)
- CIO / Head of Cloud Operations
- Legal and Compliance Representative
- Risk Management Representative

### Responsibilities:
- Review cybersecurity risks and initiatives
- Prioritize security investments
- Approve security policies and standards

## 2. Risk Management Committee
### Responsibilities:
- Review enterprise and cloud-specific risks
- Approve risk treatment decisions
- Escalate significant risks to executive management

## 3. Board Oversight
The Board receives:
- Quarterly cybersecurity risk reports
- Incident summaries
- Program maturity updates
