# Recovery Objectives (RTO & RPO)

| System / Service | RTO | RPO |
|------------------|-----|-----|
| Cloud Management Platform | 4 hours | 15 minutes |
| Customer Support Systems | 8 hours | 1 hour |
| Internal Reporting Systems | 24 hours | 12 hours |

## Governance
RTO and RPO values are approved by business owners and reviewed annually.
