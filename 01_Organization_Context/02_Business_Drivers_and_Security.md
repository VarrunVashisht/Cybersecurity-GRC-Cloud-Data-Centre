# Business Drivers and Cybersecurity Alignment

## 1. Key Business Drivers
- Revenue growth through enterprise cloud adoption
- Regulatory compliance to serve finance and healthcare clients
- Operational resilience and uptime guarantees
- Customer trust and long-term contracts

## 2. Cybersecurity Alignment
Cybersecurity enables the business by:
- Protecting sensitive customer data from breaches
- Ensuring availability of cloud platforms
- Supporting regulatory certifications and audits
- Reducing financial and operational risk

## 3. Cybersecurity as a Business Enabler
Strong governance, risk management, and compliance practices allow Q-Day Cloud Services to:
- Enter regulated markets
- Win enterprise and government contracts
- Demonstrate security maturity to customers and auditors
