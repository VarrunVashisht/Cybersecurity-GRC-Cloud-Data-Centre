## 4. Business Objectives

- Maintain customer trust and data confidentiality
- Meet regulatory and compliance obligations

## 5. Why Cybersecurity Is Critical
As a cloud service provider, Q-Day Cloud Services is responsible for protecting sensitive customer data, ensuring service availability, and maintaining compliance with multiple regulatory frameworks. A failure in cybersecurity could lead to regulatory penalties, customer loss, and reputational damage.
