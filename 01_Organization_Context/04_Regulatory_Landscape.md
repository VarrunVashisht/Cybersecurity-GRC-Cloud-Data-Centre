# Regulatory and Compliance Landscape

## 1. Applicable Regulations
- Data protection and privacy regulations
- Financial industry security requirements
- Healthcare data protection obligations
- Government cloud security expectations

## 2. Key Compliance Drivers
- Protection of customer data
- Secure cloud service delivery
- Audit and regulatory reporting requirements

## 3. Impact on GRC Program
Compliance requirements influence:
- Security control selection
- Risk assessment priorities
- Audit and reporting processes
- Incident response and breach notification
