# Third-Party Risk Management (TPRM) Program Overview

## 1. Purpose
To identify, assess, manage, and monitor cybersecurity risks introduced by third-party vendors and service providers.

## 2. Scope
This program applies to all vendors that:
- Access Q-Day Cloud Services systems or data
- Support critical business functions
- Provide cloud, SaaS, or managed services

## 3. Objectives
- Reduce supply chain risk
- Protect customer and business data
- Meet regulatory and contractual obligations
- Ensure accountability for third-party security
