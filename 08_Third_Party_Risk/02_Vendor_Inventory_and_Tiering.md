# Vendor Inventory and Risk Tiering

## 1. Vendor Inventory
All third-party vendors must be recorded in a centralized inventory.

## 2. Vendor Risk Tiers

### Tier 1 – Critical Vendors
- Access sensitive or regulated data
- Support core cloud services
- High business impact if unavailable

### Tier 2 – Important Vendors
- Limited data access
- Support important but non-critical services

### Tier 3 – Low-Risk Vendors
- No sensitive data access
- Minimal business impact

## 3. Risk Ownership
Each vendor has an assigned business owner responsible for risk decisions.
