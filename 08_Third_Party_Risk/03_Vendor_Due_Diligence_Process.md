# Vendor Due Diligence Process

## Step 1: Vendor Identification
Identify vendor services, data access, and system integration.

## Step 2: Risk Tier Assignment
Classify vendor based on data sensitivity and business criticality.

## Step 3: Security Assessment
Conduct cybersecurity questionnaires, document reviews, and control evaluations.

## Step 4: Risk Evaluation
Assess findings and determine residual risk.

## Step 5: Approval
Obtain formal approval before onboarding.
