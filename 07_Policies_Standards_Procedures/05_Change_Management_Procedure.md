# Change Management Procedure

## Step 1: Change Request
Document the proposed change, scope, and justification.

## Step 2: Risk Assessment
Assess security and operational risks associated with the change.

## Step 3: Approval
Obtain approval from appropriate stakeholders.

## Step 4: Implementation
Implement change following approved plan.

## Step 5: Validation
Verify successful implementation and no adverse impact.

## Step 6: Documentation
Record change outcomes and retain evidence.
