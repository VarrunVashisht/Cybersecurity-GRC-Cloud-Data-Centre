# Risk Management Policy

## 1. Purpose
To ensure cybersecurity risks are identified, assessed, treated, and monitored consistently.

## 2. Risk Management Approach
Q-Day Cloud Services uses a structured risk management framework aligned with NIST and enterprise governance requirements.

## 3. Risk Ownership
Business owners are accountable for risks affecting their services.

## 4. Risk Acceptance
Risk acceptance must align with the approved risk appetite and be formally documented.

## 5. Review
Risks are reviewed periodically based on severity.
