# Incident Response Procedure

## Step 1: Detection
Identify and report suspected security incidents.

## Step 2: Analysis
Assess scope, impact, and severity.

## Step 3: Containment
Limit the spread and impact of the incident.

## Step 4: Eradication
Remove malicious components.

## Step 5: Recovery
Restore systems to normal operation.

## Step 6: Lessons Learned
Document findings and improvement actions.
