# Information Security Policy

## 1. Purpose
To establish the overarching information security requirements for protecting Q-Day Cloud Services information assets.

## 2. Scope
This policy applies to all employees, contractors, systems, cloud services, and third-party providers.

## 3. Policy Statement
Q-Day Cloud Services is committed to protecting the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information through risk-based security controls and continuous monitoring.

## 4. Roles and Responsibilities
- Board: Oversight and accountability
- Executive Management: Enforcement and resourcing
- CISO: Policy ownership and implementation
- Employees: Policy compliance

## 5. Compliance
Non-compliance may result in disciplinary action or contract termination.
