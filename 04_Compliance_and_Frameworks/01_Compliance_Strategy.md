# Compliance Strategy

## 1. Purpose
This document defines the compliance approach used by Q-Day Cloud Services to meet regulatory, contractual, and customer security requirements.

## 2. Compliance Philosophy
Q-Day Cloud Services adopts a risk-based and control-centric approach to compliance by:
- Using NIST CSF as the primary framework
- Mapping additional frameworks to existing controls
- Avoiding duplicated or conflicting controls

## 3. Key Compliance Objectives
- Demonstrate security maturity to customers
- Meet regulatory and audit requirements
- Support secure cloud service delivery
- Reduce compliance costs through control reuse
