# Compliance Evidence and Audit Readiness

## 1. Types of Evidence
- Policies and procedures
- System configurations
- Logs and monitoring reports
- Risk assessments and approvals
- Incident response records

## 2. Evidence Management
- Evidence is maintained centrally
- Version control is enforced
- Access to evidence is restricted

## 3. Audit Readiness
- Periodic internal reviews
- Pre-audit gap assessments
- Continuous improvement based on audit findings
