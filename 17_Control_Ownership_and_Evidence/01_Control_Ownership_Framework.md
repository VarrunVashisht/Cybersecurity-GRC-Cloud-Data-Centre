# Control Ownership Framework

## Purpose
To establish clear accountability for cybersecurity controls across Q-Day Cloud Services.

## Control Owner Responsibilities
- Ensure control is designed appropriately
- Ensure control operates effectively
- Produce and maintain evidence
- Address control deficiencies

## Control Categories
- Administrative controls
- Technical controls
-ISK management review 

## Ownership Principles
- Every control has exactly one primary owner
- Ownership aligns with operational responsibility
- GRC provides oversight, not execution
