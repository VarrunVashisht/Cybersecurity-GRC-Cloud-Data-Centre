# Control to Tool to Evidence Mapping

| Control | Tool/System | Evidence Produced | Evidence Location | Frequency |
|-------|-------------|-------------------|------------------|-----------|
| Access Control | IAM | Access logs, approvals | Secure repository | Monthly |
| Logging | SIEM | Log retention reports | SIEM dashboard | Continuous |
| Incident Response | IR Platform | Incident reports | IR system | Per incident |
| Vendor Risk | TPRM Tool | Risk assessments | GRC system | Annually |
| Change Mgmt | ITSM | Change tickets | ITSM system | Per change |
