# Breach Scenario Definition

## Scenario Summary
A misconfigured cloud storage service resulted in unauthorized access to customer data over a 10-day period before detection.

## Data Impacted
- Customer names and email addresses
- Limited account metadata
- No payment card data confirmed

## Systems Affected
- Cloud Management Platform (CMP)
- Centralized logging services

## Discovery Method
Detected through automated monitoring alerts and SOC investigation.
