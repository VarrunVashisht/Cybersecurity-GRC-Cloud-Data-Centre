# Customer Breach Notification

## What Happened
We identified unauthorized access to one of our cloud storage systems.

## What Information Was Involved
Limited account-related information. No passwords or payment data were affected.

## What We Did
- Secured the system immediately
- Enhanced monitoring
- Initiated a full security review

## What You Can Do
Remain vigilant and report suspicious activity.

## Our Commitment
We take security seriously and are committed to transparency.
