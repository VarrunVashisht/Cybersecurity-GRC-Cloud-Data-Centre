# Cybersecurity Audit Charter

## 1. Purpose
To provide independent assurance that cybersecurity controls at Q-Day Cloud Services are designed and operating effectively.

## 2. Independence
Audit activities are performed independently from operational and GRC functions.

## 3. Scope
Audits may include:
- Governance and risk management
- Cloud security controls
- Incident response
- Third-party risk management
- Compliance alignment

## 4. Authority
Auditors are authorized to access systems, documentation, and personnel required for audit activities.
