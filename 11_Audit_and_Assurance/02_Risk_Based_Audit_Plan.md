# Risk-Based Cybersecurity Audit Plan

## 1. Audit Methodology
Audit scope and depth are determined based on risk levels and business impact.

## 2. High-Risk Areas
- Identity and access management
- Cloud configuration management
- Incident response readiness
- Third-party access controls

## 3. Audit Frequency
- High-risk areas: Annually
- Medium-risk areas: Biennially
- Low-risk areas: As required
