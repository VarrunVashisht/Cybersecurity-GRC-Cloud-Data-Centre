# Risk Scoring Methodology

## 1. Likelihood Scale
- Low (1): Rare occurrence
- Medium (2): Possible occurrence
- High (3): Likely occurrence

## 2. Impact Scale
- Low (1): Minimal operational impact
- Medium (2): Service disruption or limited data exposure
- High (3): Regulatory impact or significant data breach

## 3. Risk Rating Formula
Risk Score = Likelihood × Impact

## 4. Risk Levels
- 1–2: Low
- 3–4: Medium
- 6–9: High
