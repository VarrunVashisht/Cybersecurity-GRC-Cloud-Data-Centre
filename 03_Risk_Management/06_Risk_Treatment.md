# Risk Treatment and Acceptance

## 1. Risk Treatment Options
- Mitigate: Implement controls to reduce risk
- Transfer: Shift risk through contracts or insurance
- Accept: Formally accept risk within tolerance
- Avoid: Discontinue risky activities

## 2. Risk Acceptance Process
Risk acceptance must:
- Be documented
- Align with risk appetite
- Be approved by appropriate authority
- Have an expiration or review date

## 3. Risk Review Frequency
- High risks: Monthly
- Medium risks: Quarterly
- Low risks: Annually
