# Cybersecurity Risk Management Framework

## 1. Purpose
This framework defines the approach used by Q-Day Cloud Services to identify, assess, treat, and monitor cybersecurity risks across cloud infrastructure and operations.

## 2. Scope
This framework applies to:
- Cloud infrastructure and services
- Customer and internal data
- Third-party providers
- Employees and contractors

## 3. Risk Management Objectives
- Protect confidentiality, integrity, and availability
- Reduce likelihood and impact of cyber incidents
- Support regulatory and compliance requirements
- Enable informed risk-based decision-making

## 4. Risk Management Life Cycle
- Risk Identification
- Risk Analysis
- Risk Evaluation
- Risk Treatment
- Risk Monitoring and Reporting
