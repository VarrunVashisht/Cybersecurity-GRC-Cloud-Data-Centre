# Intentionally Unsecured or Partially Secured Areas

## Area 1: Legacy Internal Reporting Tools
- Reason: Scheduled for decommission
- Risk: Limited internal data exposure
- Compensating Controls: Restricted access, enhanced logging
- Review Date: 6 months

## Area 2: Non-Critical Development Environments
- Reason: Resource prioritization toward production
- Risk: Low business impact
- Compensating Controls: Network isolation, no customer data
- Review Date: Quarterly

## Area 3: Advanced Threat Hunting
- Reason: Cost vs benefit assessment
- Risk: Reduced detection of sophisticated threats
- Compensating Controls: Strong baseline monitoring
- Review Date: Annual
