# GRC Roles and Cross-Team Interactions

## GRC Team
- Owns frameworks, risk processes, and compliance mapping
- Coordinates audits and assessments
- Reports risk to executives

## Cloud & IT Operations
- Implements technical controls
- Responds to findings and incidents
- Provides evidence

## SOC / Security Operations
- Monitors alerts and incidents
- Feeds incidents and trends into risk management

## Legal & Compliance
- Advises on regulatory obligations
- Supports breach notification and contracts

## Business Units
- Own risks related to their services
- Approve risk acceptance decisions
