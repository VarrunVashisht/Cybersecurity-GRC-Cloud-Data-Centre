# GRC Activity Cadence

## Daily Activities
- Review security alerts impacting risk posture
- Track new incidents or vulnerabilities
- Respond to risk-related queries

## Weekly Activities
- Update risk register
- Review third-party risk changes
- Follow up on remediation actions

## Monthly Activities
- Metrics and dashboard reporting
- Control effectiveness review
- Compliance status check

## Quarterly Activities
- Risk reassessments
- Board and executive reporting
- Tabletop exercises or reviews
